Acme GmbH is a mid-sized B2B software company headquartered in Munich, serving clients across the DACH region.

We are looking for a Senior Software Engineer (m/w/d) to join our backend platform team.

The Role

As a Senior Software Engineer you will design and implement scalable backend services, collaborate with product and data teams, and mentor junior engineers. You will take technical ownership of core platform components and help define our engineering standards.

What You'll Do

    Design and build robust, well-tested REST and gRPC services in Python and Go
    Lead technical discussions, write architecture decision records, and review code
    Collaborate with Product Management and UX to refine requirements and acceptance criteria
    Contribute to CI/CD pipelines, observability, and infrastructure-as-code (Terraform, Kubernetes)
    Mentor junior and mid-level engineers through code reviews and pair programming

Requirements

    5+ years of professional software engineering experience
    Strong proficiency in Python (FastAPI, SQLAlchemy) and at least one statically typed language (Go, Java, or Kotlin)
    Solid understanding of relational databases (PostgreSQL) and message brokers (Kafka or RabbitMQ)
    Experience building and operating distributed systems in a cloud environment (AWS or GCP)
    Fluent written and spoken German (B2 or above); English at professional working level

Nice to Have

    Experience with LLM integration or AI-assisted product features
    Background in platform engineering or internal developer tooling
    Contributions to open-source projects
    Familiarity with the DACH hiring market and German labour law basics

We offer a competitive salary, flexible remote/hybrid working, 30 days holiday, and a clear path to Staff Engineer or Engineering Lead.

Acme GmbH is an equal-opportunity employer committed to building a diverse and inclusive team.
